# Digital Humanities Social Cultural Analysis

## Overview
This skill library provides comprehensive tools for analyzing social and cultural dimensions of digital humanities data. It focuses on social media analysis, cultural pattern recognition, and societal impact assessment through computational methods.

## Core Competencies

### Social Media Analysis
- Sentiment analysis of social media content
- Network extraction and analysis from social connections
- Trend detection across social platforms
- Audience analysis and demographic profiling
- Content categorization and thematic mapping

### Cultural Pattern Recognition
- Identification of cultural motifs in digital content
- Analysis of cultural evolution over time
- Cross-cultural comparison through digital artifacts
- Detection of cultural influence patterns
- Mapping of cultural networks and relationships

### Societal Impact Assessment
- Analysis of digital media's impact on public opinion
- Measurement of cultural diffusion through digital channels
- Assessment of societal trends through computational methods
- Evaluation of digital humanities projects' social impact
- Prediction of cultural shifts based on digital data

## Usage
This library is designed to be integrated into larger digital humanities projects or used as a standalone tool for social and cultural analysis. The scripts can be imported as modules or run directly with sample data.

## Applications
- Analyzing public reception of literary works on social media
- Studying cultural trends through digital communication
- Mapping intellectual networks in humanities research
- Assessing the societal impact of digital humanities projects
- Understanding cultural evolution through computational analysis
