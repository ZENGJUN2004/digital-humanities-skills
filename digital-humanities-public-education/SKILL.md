# Digital Humanities Public Education

## Overview
This skill library provides comprehensive tools for public education in the digital humanities. It focuses on digital storytelling, educational content management, curriculum design, community engagement, and educational technology integration through computational methods.

## Core Competencies

### Digital Storytelling
- Creation and management of digital narratives
- Multimedia storytelling techniques
- Interactive storytelling experiences
- Digital narrative analysis and evaluation
- Cross-platform storytelling strategies

### Educational Content Management
- Curriculum design and development for digital humanities
- Learning object creation and management
- Educational resource organization and categorization
- Content adaptation for different learning environments
- Educational content assessment and evaluation

### Community Engagement
- Community-based digital humanities projects
- Public programming and outreach
- Citizen scholarship and participatory research
- Community data collection and management
- Digital humanities for community development

### Educational Technology Integration
- Educational technology selection and implementation
- Learning management system integration
- Educational data analytics
- Adaptive learning technologies
- Accessibility and inclusive design for educational technologies

### Pedagogical Approaches
- Active learning strategies for digital humanities
- Inquiry-based learning methodologies
- Collaborative learning techniques
- Experiential learning opportunities
- Assessment and evaluation strategies

This library is designed to be integrated into larger educational projects or used as a standalone tool for educational content management. The scripts can be imported as modules or run directly with sample data.

- Developing community engagement programs in digital humanities

- Evaluating the effectiveness of digital humanities educational initiatives
